# Business Questions and KPI Framework

## Overview

This document defines the business objectives, stakeholder requirements, key performance indicators (KPIs), and business questions addressed by the Power BI reporting layer of the Olist Decision Intelligence Platform.

The purpose is to ensure that every dashboard visual directly supports business decision-making.

---

# Dashboard 1: Executive Overview

## Stakeholders

* CEO
* Executive Leadership Team
* Business Managers

## Objective

Provide a high-level overview of overall business performance, growth trends, and revenue drivers.

## Business Questions

1. How much revenue has the business generated?
2. How many orders have been placed?
3. How many customers have made purchases?
4. What is the average value of each order?
5. How are revenue and order volumes changing over time?
6. Is the business growing or declining?
7. Which regions contribute the most revenue?
8. Which product categories generate the highest sales?
9. What are the major revenue drivers?
10. Are there seasonal patterns in sales performance?

## KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Revenue Growth %
* Order Growth %

---

# Dashboard 2: Logistics Performance

## Stakeholders

* Operations Manager
* Logistics Team
* Supply Chain Management

## Objective

Monitor delivery efficiency and identify operational bottlenecks affecting customer experience.

## Business Questions

1. What is the average delivery time?
2. What percentage of orders are delivered late?
3. How many delay days occur on average?
4. Which regions experience the longest delivery times?
5. Which sellers contribute most to delivery delays?
6. Which product categories experience the highest delays?
7. How are delivery delays trending over time?
8. Which regions have poor logistics performance?
9. What proportion of orders are delivered on time?
10. Where are operational inefficiencies occurring?

## KPIs

* Average Delivery Time
* Delay Rate %
* Average Delay Days
* On-Time Delivery Rate %
* Late Orders

---

# Dashboard 3: Customer Experience

## Stakeholders

* Customer Experience Team
* Customer Success Managers
* Business Managers

## Objective

Evaluate customer satisfaction, loyalty, and factors influencing customer experience.

## Business Questions

1. What is the overall customer satisfaction level?
2. How are review scores trending over time?
3. What proportion of customers leave positive or negative reviews?
4. Which product categories receive the lowest ratings?
5. Which sellers consistently receive poor reviews?
6. Does delivery delay affect customer satisfaction?
7. Which regions have the most dissatisfied customers?
8. How many customers make repeat purchases?
9. What is the repeat purchase rate?
10. Which factors are associated with low customer satisfaction?

## KPIs

* Average Review Score
* Positive Review Rate %
* Negative Review Rate %
* Repeat Purchase Rate %
* Number of Repeat Customers

---

# Dashboard 4: Seller Performance

## Stakeholders

* Marketplace Operations Team
* Seller Relationship Managers
* Executive Team

## Objective

Evaluate seller contribution, operational efficiency, and business risk.

## Business Questions

1. Which sellers generate the highest revenue?
2. Which sellers process the highest order volumes?
3. Which sellers have the highest delay rates?
4. Which sellers consistently receive poor customer reviews?
5. Which sellers are top performers overall?
6. Which sellers present operational risks?
7. How does seller performance vary across regions?
8. Which sellers show declining performance trends?
9. What proportion of revenue comes from top-performing sellers?
10. Which sellers require intervention or support?

## KPIs

* Seller Revenue
* Orders per Seller
* Seller Delay Rate %
* Average Seller Review Score
* Seller Risk Score
